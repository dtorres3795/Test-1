About This Tool

My website creates strong, completely random passwords to help keep your accounts secure. Each password is 16 characters long and uses a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

Why Strong Passwords Matter

Weak passwords are one of the most common reasons of account breaches. A strong password should be long and contain unique characters. Avoid reusing passwords across multiple accounts/platforms.

How It Works

The generator selects from a list of different characters and uses JavaScript's Math.random() to create a unique, completely randomized password. Every click produces 6 completely new passwords instantly.
