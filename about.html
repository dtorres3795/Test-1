About This Tool

This password generator creates strong, random passwords to help keep your accounts secure. Each password is 16 characters long and uses a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

Why Strong Passwords Matter

Weak passwords are one of the most common causes of account breaches. A strong password should be long, random, and unique for every site you use. Never reuse passwords across multiple accounts.

How It Works

The generator pulls from an array of 90+ characters and uses JavaScript's Math.random() to build each password character by character. Every click produces 6 completely new passwords instantly.
